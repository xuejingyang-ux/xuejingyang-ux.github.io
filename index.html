Jingyang Xue is an undergraduate student in the School of Computer Science and Technology at Qingdao University, majoring in Computer Science and Technology. His research focuses on multimodal large models and artificial intelligence, with recent work on vision-language model interpretability, brain-model alignment, multimodal trajectory optimization, and embodied AI.

薛憬阳目前就读于青岛大学计算机科学与技术学院，研究兴趣集中在多模态大模型与人工智能方向。近期科研工作包括视觉语言模型的神经认知动态分析、脑-模型对齐、多模态动静场景轨迹优化，以及具身任务时空推理。
