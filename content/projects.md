### Multimodal Trajectory Optimization for Dynamic and Static Scenes

Core member, National College Student Innovation and Entrepreneurship Training Program.

This project addresses unstable camera parameter estimation, trajectory drift, and poor temporal continuity in dynamic scenes with moving objects, illumination changes, occlusion, and low-texture regions. Built on SLAM pose-annotated datasets such as RealEstate10K, it integrates RGB images, camera poses, and visual-inertial information. The framework uses Qwen2.5-VL / Transformer as the backbone and combines pretraining, SLAM knowledge distillation, contrastive optimization, LoRA fine-tuning, bundle adjustment, pose graph optimization, and differentiable Ceres for end-to-end trajectory optimization.
